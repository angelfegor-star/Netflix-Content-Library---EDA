# Netflix-Content-Library---EDA

## Questions Explored
1. How has Netflix's content library grown year over year?
2. Which countries dominate the catalogue?
3. What audience does the ratings distribution reveal?

## Key Findings
1. Content additions peaked in 2019, TV Shows growing as a share of total titles
2. Top 3 countries account for 57.3% of all content despite Netflix operating in 190+ countries
3. 45.5% of content is adult-rated, implying Netflix is primarily an adult entertainment platform, not a family service
